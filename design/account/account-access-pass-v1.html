5 Downgrade to Basic Access Pass (Confirmation Modal)
Triggered when a user with a paid pass selects Free and clicks Continue. Lists the consequences of downgrading. “Cancel” dismisses; “Yes, Downgrade” executes.
Downgrade to Basic Access Pass?
You are about to downgrade to the Basic Access Pass. Your current benefits will remain active until your access pass expires.
What happens now
  • Your autopay subscription will not renew at the next billing cycle.
  • You will keep your Advanced Access Pass benefits until your current pass ends on January 1, 2025.
After your access pass ends on January 1, 2025
  • You will lose the ability to book beyond 30 days.
  • Any sessions scheduled past 30 days from that date will be unscheduled and returned to your account.
Are you sure you want to continue?
6 Upgrade to Advanced Access Pass — Monthly (Confirmation Modal)
Triggered when a user on the Free/Basic plan selects Monthly and clicks Continue. The modal summarises the subscription they are about to start: price, billing frequency, and the benefits they will gain. “Cancel” dismisses; “Yes, Upgrade” executes.
Upgrade to Advanced Access Pass?
You are about to subscribe to the Advanced Access Pass. This means:
Subscription details
  • You will be charged $9.00 / Monthly starting today.
  • Your subscription will auto-renew each month until canceled.
  • You can cancel your Advanced Access Pass anytime from this page.
Benefits you will gain
  • Book out past 30 days.
  • Up to 5 students can share your Advanced Access Pass.
  • Lock in your current rate as long as you have sessions.
7 Upgrade to Advanced Access Pass — Yearly (Confirmation Modal)
Same as Section 5 but for the Yearly billing period. The modal highlights the annual price and the savings compared to monthly billing.
Upgrade to Advanced Access Pass?
You are about to subscribe to the Advanced Access Pass. This means:
Subscription details
  • You will be charged $90.00 / Yearly ($7.50 / month) starting today.
  • Your subscription will auto-renew each year until canceled.
  • You can cancel your Advanced Access Pass anytime from this page.
Benefits you will gain
  • Book out past 30 days.
  • Up to 5 students can share your Advanced Access Pass.
  • Lock in your current rate as long as you have sessions.
You save $18.00/year compared to monthly billing
8 Switch Billing Period — Monthly → Yearly (Confirmation Modal)
User already has an active Monthly plan and selects Yearly. The modal explains the billing change: prorated credit from remaining monthly period, new yearly charge, and the savings.
Switch to Yearly billing?
You are about to change your Advanced Access Pass from Monthly to Yearly billing.
Billing change
  • Your current Monthly subscription ($9.00/month) will be canceled.
  • A prorated credit of $6.00 will be applied for the remaining 20 days of your current billing cycle.
  • You will be charged $84.00 today, and then $90.00 / Yearly starting January 1, 2027.
