1b Desktop — My Students (Compact Variation)
Restructured around the Party → Package model from the domain docs. Credits are assigned to Parties (the group of attendees), not directly to individual accounts. A single-student Party = an individual card; a two-student Party = a paired card. This eliminates the V1 duplication problem where paired credits appeared on both students’ cards (looking like 8 total when only 4 exist). Session type (individual / paired) is now implicit from the card type — solo cards have one avatar, pair cards have overlapping avatars + a purple left-border accent — so chips just show count × duration with no redundant “individual” / “paired” label. Convert and Reassign remain in the overflow menu. The pair card’s dropdown omits Edit / Delete Student (those are per-account, not per-party) and shows the expanded menu.
