4 Interaction — Staged Deletions & Primary Change (Unsaved)
When the user presses the trash icon on a list item or clicks "Set primary" on a non-primary email, the change is staged locally but not yet committed to the server. The item remains visible with a distinct visual treatment so the user understands what will happen when they hit "Save Changes". The trash button is replaced with an "Undo" action to revert the staged change. This state applies uniformly to emails, phones, and connected accounts.

Pending removal: Red dashed border, faded background, strikethrough text, "Will be removed" badge, undo button.
Pending primary change: Only verified emails may be set as primary. The target email gets a dashed blue border with "Will become primary" badge; the current primary gets a dashed orange border with "Will lose primary" badge.

Profile Information
Delete your account?

You are about to permanently delete your HSA account. This action cannot be undone and will take effect immediately.

What will happen
  • All upcoming classes will be cancelled and any remaining credits will be forfeited.
  • Your class history, student profiles, and progress data will be permanently erased.
  • Any active subscriptions or payment plans will be terminated. No refunds will be issued for the current billing period.
  • Connected third-party accounts (Google, etc.) will be unlinked.
  • You will be signed out of all devices immediately.
8 Modal — Delete Confirmed (DELETE typed, button active)
Once the user types "DELETE" in the confirmation field, the destructive action button becomes active (red). Clicking it will permanently delete the account.
